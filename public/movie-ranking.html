The Best Movies According to Fathima

My top 3 movies of all-time

Om Shanti Om

My favorite childhood film. The songs are amazing and I just love SRK-Deepika. In my opinion, OSO is one of the finest Bollywood movies of the 2000s era.

Tangled

My favorite Disney Princess movie. I love Rapunzal's character(she's my fav Disney princess obviously) and all the other characters are great as well especially Eugene/Flynn Rider. Their romance is so sweet and the soundtrack is also really good. I especially adore "I see the light".

Barbie (2023)

Just a really fun and colorful movie :)
